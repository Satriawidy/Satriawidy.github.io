Personal Website

My name is Satria Widyanto and I am an aspiring theoretical physicist.

I obtained my MSc degree in theoretical physics from the University of Edinburgh. I did a dissertation project on "Feasibility Study to Search for Higgs Boson Decays to Gluons Using Machine Learning at the LHC", where I analysed the simulated events related to $H \rightarrow gg$ process and study the feasibility of observing the process in the upcoming high-luminosity (HL) phase of the LHC. During the analysis, I used several machine learning frameworks, such as graph neural networks and boosted decision trees.

I am passionate about teaching and have strong interest in using computer program for solving physics problem. During my undergraduate degree, I worked as teaching assistant for several courses, including mechanics, statistical physics, and introduction to particle physics. Recently, aside from applying machine learning during the dissertation project, I have also worked on several small projects, such as using differential equation solver for studying the early evolution of the universe, and studying the simple implementation of lattice QCD.
